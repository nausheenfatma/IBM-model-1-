Description :-

Input :-

English-Hindi parallel corpora is given which is sentence aligned .
Ex :- Pilgrimage is of utmost importance in Hinduism . <-> हिंदू धर्म में तीर्थ का बड़ा महत्व है .

Procedure :-

Implement the EM algorithm(IBM model 1) to find the translation probability of english-hindi word pairs (find out the word alignments)

Output :-

Translation probabilities of each word in file tr_prob.
After obtaining the translation probabilities of word pairs(english-hindi), output the top 500 sentences (English-Hindi Pair) with highest probabilities.
